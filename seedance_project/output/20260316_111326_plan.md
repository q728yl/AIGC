# Seedance Plan

## English Prompt
First Frame: @0053.png. Preserve the same battlefield composition, camera angle, and character positions from the reference image as the opening frame. Create a fast-paced fantasy combat finishing move sequence where the hero unleashes an ultimate skill and instantly kills the monster. [00s-01s] (Background Layer) Hold the battle environment exactly as seen in @0053.png with minimal parallax, keeping the battlefield stable and readable. (Action Layer) The hero begins transitioning from idle into the wind-up of the ultimate using @hero_ultimate_action_sheet_111055.png, body lowering slightly and weapon drawing power. The monster faces the hero in a brief anticipation pose before being struck. (VFX Layer) Subtle energy buildup around the hero, concentrated at the weapon edge, leading into a bright pre-impact glow. (UI Layer) Show the enemy health bar above the monster using @enemy_hp_bar_ui_111205.png, still mostly full at the start, with overall combat framing reinforced by @battle_ui_overlay_165039.png and @combat_ui_overlay_static_161821.png.
[01s-02s] (Background Layer) Keep the same scene from @0053.png but add slight camera push-in to emphasize the incoming ultimate. (Action Layer) The hero executes the main slash from @hero_ultimate_action_sheet_111055.png in a swift, decisive forward strike. The monster begins reacting with @monster_hit_reaction_sheet_111229.png, torso snapping backward from the incoming force. (VFX Layer) Burst the center impact with @ultimate_hit_vfx_sequence_111117.png layered over the contact point, enhanced by @melee_impact_vfx_sequence_161655.png and @hit_impact_smoke_vfx_sheet_114510.png. Add strong full-screen impact flash and heavy screen shake right at contact. (UI Layer) At the exact hit frame, a large red critical damage number rises above the monster’s head: use @crit_damage_minus9999_sprite_sequence_114633.png as the primary floating text, supported by @crit_damage_ui_floating_111138.png styling. The text must clearly read a red “-9999” critical hit.
[02s-03s] (Background Layer) Maintain the same battlefield while the camera continues a short residual shake, then settles. (Action Layer) The hero completes the follow-through and lands in a strong finishing pose. The monster enters a severe hit-stun state from @monster_hit_reaction_sheet_111229.png, then collapses/disintegrates. (VFX Layer) The final hit energy lingers for a few frames, then transitions into defeat smoke using @monster_death_smoke_vfx_111251.png, swallowing the monster’s body as it vanishes. (UI Layer) The enemy HP bar from @enemy_hp_bar_ui_111205.png drains instantly and completely to zero at impact, with the empty bar remaining visible for a beat. The red “-9999” continues floating upward and fading.
[03s-04s] (Background Layer) Return to a steadier camera on the original battle scene from @0053.png after the kill. (Action Layer) The hero holds a victorious post-ultimate recovery pose, calm and dominant. The monster is gone. (VFX Layer) Residual smoke and impact particles dissipate where the monster stood. (UI Layer) The enemy HP bar remains fully emptied, confirming the instant kill, while the red crit text fully fades out.
Overall style: high-impact xianxia/fantasy battle animation, crisp 2D game-combat presentation, dramatic ultimate finisher, instantaneous lethal damage, clear readability of the red “-9999” critical damage text, and obvious HP bar depletion to zero.

## Chinese Prompt
First Frame: @0053.png。开场严格保持参考图中的战场构图、镜头角度、角色站位与整体氛围，以该画面作为视频第一帧。生成一段节奏紧凑的仙侠战斗终结技动画：英雄发动必杀技，瞬间击杀怪兽。 [00s-01s] (Background Layer) 背景保持与 @0053.png 一致，战场环境基本稳定，仅有非常轻微的空间层次感，不改变原始场景信息。 (Action Layer) 英雄使用 @hero_ultimate_action_sheet_111055.png 从待机切入必杀前摇动作，身体微微下压、武器蓄力，准备爆发。怪兽面向英雄短暂僵直，形成即将被命中的预备状态。 (VFX Layer) 英雄周身与武器边缘出现克制但明显的能量聚集光效，为下一拍重击做铺垫。 (UI Layer) 在怪兽头顶显示血条 @enemy_hp_bar_ui_111205.png，初始时仍为较满状态，同时可叠加 @battle_ui_overlay_165039.png 与 @combat_ui_overlay_static_161821.png 增强战斗UI氛围。
[01s-02s] (Background Layer) 保持 @0053.png 的场景基础不变，镜头轻微推进，强化必杀即将命中的压迫感。 (Action Layer) 英雄通过 @hero_ultimate_action_sheet_111055.png 释放主斩击动作，快速、凌厉、决绝地向前爆发。怪兽立即切入 @monster_hit_reaction_sheet_111229.png 的受击动作，身体被巨大力量打得后仰。 (VFX Layer) 在命中瞬间于接触点叠加 @ultimate_hit_vfx_sequence_111117.png 作为核心爆发特效，并辅以 @melee_impact_vfx_sequence_161655.png 与 @hit_impact_smoke_vfx_sheet_114510.png 增强冲击。命中这一帧加入强烈屏幕震动与瞬间闪白，突出“必杀技命中”的重量感。 (UI Layer) 命中同帧，在怪兽头顶飘出巨大、醒目的红色暴击伤害数字，主用 @crit_damage_minus9999_sprite_sequence_114633.png，并结合 @crit_damage_ui_floating_111138.png 的浮字表现。数字必须清晰显示为红色“−9999”。
[02s-03s] (Background Layer) 战场仍维持原场景，镜头在短暂震动余波后逐步稳定。 (Action Layer) 英雄完成收招，停在强势的终结姿态。怪兽继续使用 @monster_hit_reaction_sheet_111229.png 表现重创后的僵直，随后直接崩解/倒下消失。 (VFX Layer) 命中能量残留数帧后，切换为 @monster_death_smoke_vfx_111251.png 的死亡烟雾特效，将怪兽身体吞没并抹除。 (UI Layer) 怪兽头顶的 @enemy_hp_bar_ui_111205.png 在命中瞬间直接清空到0，形成“瞬间掉空血条”的明确效果；红色“−9999”暴击数字继续上浮并逐渐淡出。
[03s-04s] (Background Layer) 镜头回归稳定，仍停留在与 @0053.png 一致的战场画面。 (Action Layer) 英雄保持必杀技后的胜利收势，姿态冷静而强大。怪兽已经消失。 (VFX Layer) 怪兽原站位处残留少量烟雾与冲击粒子，随后逐渐散去。 (UI Layer) 怪兽血条保持完全清空状态，明确传达被瞬间击杀；红色暴击数字完全淡出。
整体风格：高冲击力仙侠战斗演出、清晰的2D游戏战斗镜头语言、强烈必杀命中感、明显的屏幕震动、怪兽头顶红色“−9999”暴击伤害数字，以及血条瞬间清空的明确反馈。
