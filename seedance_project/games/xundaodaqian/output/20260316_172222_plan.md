## English Prompt
First Frame: @0001.jpg. [00s-01s] (Background Layer) Hold the composition and environment from @0001.jpg as the base battlefield, preserving the same camera angle and framing. (UI Layer) Fade in subtle combat HUD from @battle_ui_overlay_pack_172205.png at the screen edges, keeping it readable but secondary. [01s-03s] (Action Layer) The main hero bursts forward in a fast frontal dash attack using @hero_dash_attack_sheet_171953.png, moving from mid-left toward center-right with strong momentum, body leaning forward, weapon leading the motion. [03s-04s] (Action Layer) The target enemy at the forward position is struck and snaps into a hit reaction using @enemy_hit_reaction_sheet_172015.png, recoiling backward from the impact. (VFX Layer) The fire projectile connects and immediately blooms into a large explosive blast using @explosion_vfx_sheet_172112.png, expanding outward with bright orange fire, smoke, and impact flash, partially covering the enemy. [04s-05s] (VFX Layer) Let the explosion peak, then begin to dissipate into embers and smoke while the hero settles into a finishing pose after the dash.
